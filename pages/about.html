Our Story

A Passion for Excellence, A Commitment to Flavor

Since 2015

Where It All Began

Savoria was born from a simple dream: to create a dining experience that transcends the ordinary. Founded by Chef Alessandro Marchetti in 2015, our restaurant has become a beloved destination for food enthusiasts seeking authentic flavors and impeccable service.

What started as a small 20-seat establishment has grown into an award-winning restaurant, while never losing sight of our core values: quality ingredients, culinary innovation, and warm hospitality that makes every guest feel like family.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Values

Sustainability

We partner with local farmers and suppliers who share our commitment to sustainable, ethical practices. Every ingredient tells a story of care for our planet.

Passion

Our team's passion for culinary excellence is evident in every dish we serve. We don't just cook food; we create memorable experiences that touch the heart.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards in everything we do, from sourcing ingredients to plating the final dish and welcoming our guests.

Our Journey

Milestones & Memories

2015

Grand Opening

Savoria opens its doors with a 20-seat intimate dining room, focusing on seasonal Italian cuisine.

2017

First Expansion

Doubled our capacity to accommodate growing demand and added a private dining room for special events.

2019

Wine Cellar Launch

Unveiled our curated wine cellar featuring over 500 bottles from around the world.

2020

Michelin Recognition

Received our first Michelin Bib Gourmand, recognizing quality food at moderate prices.

2023

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our zero-waste kitchen program and rooftop garden, growing 30% of our herbs and vegetables.
